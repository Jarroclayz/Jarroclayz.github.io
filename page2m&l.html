CHARACTERS

Mario & Luigi: Bowsers' Inside Story - Characters

The Mushroom Kingdom has been plagued by a disease called The Blorbs, a mysterious infection that has caused the Toads to inflate like balloons and roll uncontrollably. A council meeting is immediately called at Princess Peach’s Castle, in which important figures such as Mario, Luigi, Princess Peach and Toadsworth have gathered to discuss what can be done about the epidemic. It is discovered that infected Toads had recently eaten a mushroom from a mysterious salesman.

Meanwhile, Bowser is infuriated that he had not been invited to the Council meeting. Upon his journey to Princess Peach’s council, he encounters this same salesman, who feeds him a Lucky(?) Shroom, causing Bowser to inhale uncontrollably. Upon arriving at the Castle, Bowser storms into the meeting room, inhaling the entire cast of characters, before promptly fainting.

It is now up to Bowser to uncover these mysteries, whilst Mario and Luigi try to escape the insides of Bowser’s body!

Mario & Luigi

Armed with overalls, hammers and matching hats, our iconic brothers have been sucked into Bowser's body! They must navigate through never ending nerve clusters, the gusty chest station and Bowser's flame pipe in order to escape back to the Mushroom Kingdom and find a cure for the Blorbs.

Starlow

As a representative of the star sprites, Starlow was attending Princess Peach's meeting concerning the Blorbs. She too got sucked in the Bowser's belly and now journeys with Mario and Luigi as a helpful sidekick!

Bowser

Unaware of his newfound inhabitants, Bowser awakes in Cavi Cape Cave, and begins the journey back to his castle, only to find out that Fawful is controlling the minds of all his minions. He then sets out to track down and defeat Fawful, swarmed by hordes of mechanised weaponry and Fawful's zany henchmen. However, below the surface, it seems that there are greater forces at play...

Fawful

This mysterious bean like figure is responsible for all the chaos that has plagued the mushroom kingdom. Responsible for feeding Bowser the vacuum shroom in order to get rid of the Bros, Fawful runs around causing chaos without anyone to stop him. However, it seems that his plans run far deeper than just causing mischief...

The Elite Trio

Meet Private Goomp, Corporal Paraplonk and Seargent Guy, the last of Bowser's minions that managed to escape Fawful's wrath. Occasionally bumping into Bowser, this group of goons is on their own quest; to reclaim Bowser's castle!

Dr Toadley

Adorned with a purple cloak, with Birdley by his side this all seeing Toad has been watching over the Bros and Bowser. Mario and Luigi seek his advice as he is the only one who knows the true cure for the Blorbs.

BOWSER JUNIOR'S JOURNEY

Whilst Bowser and the Bros are off on their journey, Bowser Junior is on his own quest to find a cure to the Blorbs. On his Journey he builds a powerful army of fellow minions. But his quest is quickly sabotaged as he too encounters Fawful's machines!
